Other Info

Registration
A registration fee, toiletry fee and first month's school fees are required upon registration. The toiletry fee includes supplies for the year.
School fees are due on the 1st of the month.
10% discount for paying all school fees in advance for the year.
5% discount for siblings from the same house.
3% discount for Paseo Baptist Church members.
Operating Hours
Monday - Friday
Nursery and Daycare: 7:00 - 16:30
Preschool: 7:00 - 13:00
Meals
Porridge is provided for all children from 7:00 to 8:00.
Parents must provide a nutritional snack for 10:00. An additional snack must be provided for children staying past 14:00.
Lunch is provided for daycare and nursery children. Preschool children must bring their own lunches.
Transportion
We can put you in touch with transport providers serving the center.
