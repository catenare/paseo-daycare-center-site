About Us

We are a community Christian daycare and preparatory preschool offering quality care for children aged 18 months to 6 years old. We strive to develop a Godly character through Biblical stories, verses and songs that fosters an early awareness of God’s unconditional love. Our day begins with prayer and the reading of God's Word.

We cater to the well-being of the child. This includes the emotional, social and physical aspects of the child's development. Healthy, physically active and well-nourished children are motivated to learn. We take pride in providing a clean, safe and educational environment for young children and their families.

We believe that each child is unique. We want our learners to be positive, well-balanced, confident individuals. This includes developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Our children are engaged in developmentally appropriate activities encouraging them to be life-long learners.

Other Info

Registration
A registration fee, toiletry fee and first month's school fees are required upon registration. The toiletry fee includes supplies for the year.
School fees are due on the 1st of the month.
10% discount for paying all school fees in advance for the year.
5% discount for siblings from the same house.
3% discount for Paseo Baptist Church members.
Operating Hours
Monday - Friday
Nursery and Daycare: 7:00 - 16:30
Preschool: 7:00 - 13:00
Meals
Porridge is provided for all children from 7:00 to 8:00.
Parents must provide a nutritional snack for 10:00. An additional snack must be provided for children staying past 14:00.
Lunch is provided for daycare and nursery children. Preschool children must bring their own lunches.
Transportion
We can put you in touch with transport providers serving the center.
