Paseo Daycare and Preschool

Early education based on Christian Values.

“When you asked me what I did in school today and I say, 'I just played.' Please don’t misunderstand me. For you see, I am learning as I play. I am learning to enjoy and be successful in my work. Today I am a child and my work is play.” Anita Wadley, 1974.

About Us

We are a Christian daycare and preparatory preschool offering quality care for children aged 18 months to 6 years old. We strive to develop a Godly character through Biblical stories, verses and songs and that fosters an early awareness of God’s unconditional love. Our day begins with with prayer and the reading of God's word.

We cater to the well-being of the child. This includes the emotional, social and physical aspects of the child's development. Healthy, physically active and well-nourished children are motivated to learn. We take pride in providing a clean, safe and educational environment for young children and their families.

We believe that each child is unique. We want our learners to be positive, well-balanced, confident individuals. This includes developing critical thinking and problem solving skills. Our children are engaged in developmentally appropriate activities encouraging them to be life-long learners.

Information

Registration
A registration fee, toiletry fee and first month's school fees are required upon registration. The toiletry fee includes supplies for the year.
School fees are due on the 1st of the month.
10% discount for paying all school fees in advance for the year.
5% discount for siblings from the same house.
3% discount for Paseo Baptist Church members.
Operating Hours
Monday - Friday
Nursery and Daycare: 7:00 - 16:30
Preschool: 7:00 - 13:00
Meals
Porridge is provided for all children from 7:00 to 8:00.
Parents are required to provide a nutritional snack for 10:00; this should include a fruit juice. If children stay past 14:00 then additional snack should be included as well.
Lunch is provided for Day-care and Toddlers. Pre-school children must bring their own lunches.
Transportion
Details are available for transport upon request. The Pre-school/Day-care Center does not have its own transport.
